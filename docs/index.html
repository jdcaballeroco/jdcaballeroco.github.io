Featured Project

AI Dataset Reasoning Agent

A public-facing project built around industrial dataset reasoning with LangGraph. The goal is to make early-stage dataset work more structured by guiding preprocessing, feature engineering, and baseline modeling for technical analysis workflows.

  • Problem: industrial datasets often require repetitive preprocessing and structured technical exploration before modeling becomes useful.
  • What it does: supports reasoning over datasets with a workflow that emphasizes data preparation, baseline analysis, and interpretable next steps.
  • Why it matters: it reflects a practical view of AI systems where deterministic preparation and engineering judgment are as important as model choice.
